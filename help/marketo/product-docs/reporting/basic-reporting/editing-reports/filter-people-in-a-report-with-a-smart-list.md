# 使用智慧清單篩選報表中的人員 {#filter-people-in-a-report-with-a-smart-list}

使用智慧清單來依特定人員屬性篩選報表。

您可以對下列報表類型使用智慧清單：

* [人員績效](/help/marketo/product-docs/reporting/basic-reporting/report-types/people-performance-report.md)
* [各狀態人員](/help/marketo/product-docs/reporting/basic-reporting/report-types/people-by-status-report.md)
* [各收入階段人員](/help/marketo/product-docs/reporting/revenue-cycle-analytics/revenue-tools/people-by-revenue-stage-report.md)
* [電子郵件效能](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/email-program-data/email-performance-report.md)
* [電子郵件連結效能](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/email-program-data/email-link-performance-report.md)
* [參與資料流效能](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/drip-nurturing/reports-and-notifications/engagement-stream-performance-report.md)
* [促銷活動](/help/marketo/product-docs/reporting/basic-reporting/report-types/campaign-activity-report.md)
* [促銷活動電子郵件績效](/help/marketo/product-docs/reporting/basic-reporting/report-types/campaign-email-performance-report.md)
* [公司網站活動](/help/marketo/product-docs/reporting/basic-reporting/report-types/company-web-activity-report.md)
* [網頁活動](/help/marketo/product-docs/reporting/basic-reporting/report-types/web-page-activity-report.md)

1. 前往 **行銷活動** 的上界。

   ![](assets/image2017-3-27-11-3a31-3a2.png)

1. 從導覽樹中選取您的報表，然後按一下 **智慧清單** 標籤。

   ![](assets/image2017-3-27-14-3a12-3a53.png)

1. 找到適當的篩選器，然後將其拖曳。

   ![](assets/image2017-3-27-14-3a13-3a46.png)

1. 設定篩選。

   ![](assets/image2014-9-16-12-3a35-3a50.png)

1. 按一下 **報表** 標籤來檢視您的篩選報表。

   ![](assets/image2017-3-27-14-3a14-3a16.png)

   太棒了！ 現在您的報表只會顯示符合智慧清單之人員的資料。
